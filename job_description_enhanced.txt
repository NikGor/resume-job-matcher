Frontend Developer Opportunity at Sky AI

About Sky:
At Sky, we're revolutionizing B2E AI document processing services. We believe in the power of technology to simplify complex tasks and empower businesses. Our team collaborates weekly and works asynchronously via GitHub and Slack, fostering a culture of flexibility and innovation.

Who We're Looking For:
We're seeking a talented Frontend Developer to join our diverse and inclusive team. You'll build a modern web app from scratch, work independently on its UI, and collaborate with a small, dynamic team to develop our entire product.

Tech Stack:
- React & Redux with TypeScript (Feature-Sliced Design)
- Tailwind CSS
- Shadcn's UI
- Cursor AI
- React Router
- Vite

Ideal Candidate:
- Detail-oriented with an eye for slick UI design
- Experienced in shipping features using React and TypeScript on product teams
- Thinks in terms of features and user journeys, not just code
- Understands complex state management and its product impact
- Collaborates effectively with the backend team on API design and quality

What We Offer:
- Competitive salary range: $80,000 - $120,000 USD annually (based on experience)
- Comprehensive health benefits package
- 401(k) matching program
- Professional development budget for conferences and courses
- Flexible remote work environment (EST/CEST mainly, 5-6h overlap preferred)
- Opportunity for growth into senior and leadership roles

Hiring Process:
1. Submit your resume to stas@usesky.ai
2. Initial screening call (30 minutes)
3. 1-on-1 pair programming interview with lead developer (90 minutes)
4. Meet the founders (60 minutes)
5. Offer extended within 1 week of final interview

We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace. We encourage applications from candidates of all backgrounds, including those from underrepresented groups in tech.

Sky is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic.

To Apply:
Send your resume to stas@usesky.ai with the subject line "Frontend Developer Application"

Paperwork and payments processed via Deel
